RPM

Audience#

The audience for this document is people who wish to use our setup tooling which bring significant improvements over the upstream setup scripts.

Description#

We publish our Enterprise Linux 8 tooling in our RPM repository. These are packages for software not available from upstream in package format. In some cases, such as ZFS, our packages are significantly newer.

All of the software published in our repository works on Enterprise Linux 8, whether Red Hat, CentOS, Oracle, or Rocky Linux.
